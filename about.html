Education & Career

Nsikak Umoren Uton is a consultant geomedeller and development geologist from Nto-Obio Ikang in Obot Akara. He obtained his first degree in geology from the University of Calabar and later proceeded to the prestigious imperial college, London for his Masters in petroleum geology.
